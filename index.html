 Nahi yaar  💔💔💔
        Main thik nahi hu.

Mujhe zaroorat hai teri,

tere hug ki, teri baato ki,

teri maujudgi ki.



Tu jab sang hota hai, sab kuch

thik lagne lagta hai, aur jab tu

dur hota hai to kuch thik nahi.



Mujhse akele handle nahi ho

rahi ye situation aur is waqt

mujhe teri bahut zyada zarurat

hai yaar, bahut zyada zarurat.



Tu please aa jana, tere aate hi

sab theek ho jana hai, sach mein.



Bahut baatein hain jo kehni

hain, bahut aansu hain jo

bahane hain.



Itna tuta hua mehsus aaj se

pehle kabhi nahi kiya maine.



Tu please sambhaal le yaar ❤️
